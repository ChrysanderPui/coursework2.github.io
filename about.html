What is $mart Money?

$mart Money is a simple, personal finance tracker designed to help students and clients take control of your spending, track expenses, set budgets, manage multiple currencies, and build better financial habits without ads as it is a free service.

Why is $mart Money?

$Smart money was created to provide an easy-to-use money management tool that is accessible to everyone and does not require any sign-in or account creation.

Who created $mart Money?

$Smart money was created by an individual developer who is passionate about personal finance and helping others manage their money well.
