How to get your budget on track?

Step 1

Track Your Spending

  • Record daily expenses to see where your money really goes.
  • Identify patterns and unnecessary spending.

Step 2

Set a Realistic Budget

  • Plan how much to spend, save, and keep for essentials.
  • Make sure your budget matches your lifestyle and income.

Step 3

Build Healthy Money Habits

  • Save regularly and avoid impulse spending.
  • Review and adjust your budget to stay on track.
