Help & FAQ
How do I add a new transaction?
Go to the Manager tab and use the "Add New Expense" form.
Can I set monthly budgets?
Yes! Go to the Budgets tab and set your monthly budget.
Can I use different currencies?
Yes! Go to the Multicurrency page to add and remove currencies.
How do I switch to dark mode?
Click on the "Appearance" button on this More page to turn on dark/light mode instantly.
